Plan ahead

Book intercity or special transport

Airport and railway drops, group trips, and other special transport requests are scheduled 24 hours in advance.

Good to know

  • Requests are confirmed by SMS/email within 4 hours during working hours.
  • Airport/railway drops run on a shared-shuttle model — solo late-night trips may need a separate cab booking.
  • Group trips for club events need approval from the SAC and 48 hours' notice.
  • For genuine emergencies, call the Transport Helpline directly instead of using this form.
